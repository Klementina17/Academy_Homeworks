Why I enjoy cooking?

I enjoy cooking because it lets me be creative. I love trying new recipes, mixing flavors, and making meals that taste good. Cooking also makes me feel relaxed and productive.

Tip:

Try starting with simple recipes before moving to complex ones. Watch tutorials to get inspiration!

Cooking alone or with someone?

I prefer to cook alone because it's my time to relax and enjoy the process.

Fun Fact:

Cooking with friends can be fun, but sometimes it’s nice to have your own quiet time in the kitchen.

Sweet or salty recipes?

Hmmm, I love cooking both sweet and salty recipes, but if I had to choose, I would pick sweet recipes.

Suggestion:

Try experimenting with dessert recipes—they are a fun way to practice baking and decorating.
