Fun Facts

  1. Beethoven was obsessed with coffee and counted exactly 60 beans for each cup.
  2. Espresso means “pressed out” in Italian, not “strong.
  3. Instant coffee was invented in 1901 by a Japanese scientist, Satori Kato.
  4. Before coffee was brewed, Ethiopians mixed coffee cherries with fat to make an energy snack.
  5. Dark roast has slightly less caffeine than light roast because caffeine burns off during roasting.
