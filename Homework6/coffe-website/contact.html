What is the strongest type of coffee?

Ristretto and espresso are the strongest, while Robusta beans naturally have more caffeine.

Is coffee healthy?

Yes! In moderation, coffee can boost energy, improve focus, and provide antioxidants.

What is the best way to store coffee beans?

Keep beans in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Avoid the fridge and freezer.
